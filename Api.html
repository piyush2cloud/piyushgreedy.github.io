Benefits

Higher Revenue
With money-pooling barrier removed, high ticket and high margin products (or campaigns - for crowdfunding) can be taken with ease. Hence, the group paying power leads to an increase in the Average Revenue/Contribution per customer.
Zero CoCA
Since the plugin is inherently viral and allows people to invite group members on the site itself via e-mail or Facebook, hence there is an increase in the number of customers (many of them tend to be first timers!) at almost zero expenditure.
Increased Conversions
Since the payment step now involves an easy concept of sharing the money on the site itself, hence the group members don't drop off elsewhere to make decisions or collections for that purpose.
Time Saved
Not only do the site customers save time, but you too save a lot of energy and precious moments as a business by not chasing down multi-party payments like DD, cheques and Cash requests.
Better Engagement
Having this as a feature on your site helps you stand out in this competitive race, hence fostering more loyalty and better engagement from your end users, leading to primarily long term benefits.
